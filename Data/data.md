### WeatherDataVec
Stores meteorological data from 1980 to 2020
Features used :
1. Precipitation
2. Temperature
3. Wind_x(x-component of wind vector)
4. Wind_y(y-component of wind vector)
5. Relative Humidity
6. Atmospheric Pressure
7. Visibility
8. Stage Height(ft)

### Scale_var.pkl
Stores standard deviation and mean for normalizing input data
